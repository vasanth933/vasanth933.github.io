01. Project Overview

InstaFabrics is a revolutionary mobile marketplace connecting fabric suppliers with boutiques and tailoring businesses. The app allows users to browse, search, and purchase fabrics from verified suppliers with real-time inventory management and secure payment processing.

I architected and developed the complete backend infrastructure, including RESTful APIs, real-time notifications, payment integration, and a sophisticated recommendation engine that increased user engagement by 45%.
